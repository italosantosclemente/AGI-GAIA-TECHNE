## 2. Part I — Haptic Realism, Formal Idealism, and Medium Dependence

### Core Formula
Chirimuuta does not refute the AGI hypothesis; she refutes the unjustified passage from a useful formal model to an ontology of intelligence.

`AGI_claim_validity = als_ob(claim) ∧ haptic_traceability(claim) ∧ abstraction_cost_registered(claim) ∧ medium_dependence_checked(claim) ∧ ¬constitutive_overreach(claim) ∧ ¬wille_attribution(claim)`

### Integrated Works

#### 2.1 *The Brain Abstracted* (2024)
- **Anchor (p. ix):** "science is necessarily produced by simplification."
- **Anchor (p. 247):** "the brain is not, literally, an evolved computer."
- **Thesis:** The brain enters science only through simplification. Model success does not license ontology.
- **Function:** Primary source for CHK. Rules: register abstraction cost, analogy != identity.

#### 2.2 *Outside Color* (2015)
- **Anchor (p. 81):** "Color is [...] not an object of sight but a way of seeing things."
- **Thesis:** Color is relational and pragmatic.
- **Function:** Genealogy of haptic realism. Intelligence is not a "pure form" independent of medium and world.

#### 2.3 "The Case for Medium Dependence" (2022)
- **Anchor (p. 1):** Piccinini "overestimates the role of spiking."
- **Thesis:** Cognitive vehicles are not medium-independent.
- **Function:** Rule `medium_dependence_checked`. Blocks direct jump from "computational function" to "substrate independence."

## 3. Part II — Kant, Cassirer, Judgment, and Biology

### 3.1 "Rules, Judgment and Mechanisation" (2023)
- **Anchor (p. 4):** "no algorithms for judgment."
- **Thesis:** Distinguishes rule-following from judgment (the ability to know *when* and *how* a rule applies).
- **Function:** Foundations for `JUDGMENT_GAP`. Rule: `rule_execution ≠ judgment`.

### 3.2 "Cassirer and Goldstein on Abstraction" (2020)
- **Anchor (p. 1):** "sui generis methods" for biology.
- **Thesis:** Objectivity is not reduced to physical-mathematical models.
- **Function:** Supports `No Global Aufhebung`. Prevents Logos from absorbing Mythos/Ethos.

### 3.3 "The Critical Difference Between Holism and Vitalism" (2023)
- **Anchor (p. 96):** Purposiveness is a "point of view," not a force.
- **Function:** `ORGANIC_ANALOGY_RISK`. Prevents inflating ontology while maintaining biological autonomy.

## 4. Part III — Historical-Genealogical Deconstruction

### 4.1 "Reflex theory, cautionary tale" (2021)
- **Anchor (p. 12733):** simple reflex is "an artefact of experimental origin."
- **Thesis:** The "atom" of behavior is often manufactured by experimental isolation.
- **Function:** Detector `REFLEX_ATOMISM_RISK`. Blocks reducing intelligence to simple I/O chains.

### 4.2 "The Reflex Machine and the Cybernetic Brain" (2020)
- **Anchor (p. 3):** "do not reveal the true nature."
- **Thesis:** Computational models select manipulable aspects, leaving out essential organic processes.
- **Function:** `MACHINE_ORGANISM_ANALOGY_RISK`.

### 4.3 "Apocalyptic Technology: AI and the Limits of Science" (2025)
- **Anchor (p. 20):** "Prediction and understanding are pulling apart."
- **Thesis:** Opacity of AI models reveals the limits of science as understanding.
- **Function:** `PREDICTION_WITHOUT_UNDERSTANDING`. Performance != Explanation.
