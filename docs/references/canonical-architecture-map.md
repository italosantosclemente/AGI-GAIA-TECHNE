# Canonical Architecture Map: Unified Prism-Werk

This document defines the canonical architecture and terminology for the AGI-GAIA-TECHNE framework.

## The Three Triads

The architecture is structured around three distinct but interrelated triads. These must never be collapsed or confused.

| Layer | Terms | Status | Rule |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cassirerian symbolic functions** | Ausdruck / Darstellung / Bedeutung | Functional dimensions | Never containers, never mutually exclusive. |
| **Symbolic-cultural profiles** | Mythos / Sprache / Wissenschaft | Qualitative accents | Each contains all three functions by dominant accent. |
| **Clemente operational fields** | Mythos / Logos / Ethos | Runtime orchestration fields | Mythos anchors, Logos articulates, Ethos tracks limits. |

### The Golden Rule of Non-Identity

**Never say:** Mythos = Ausdruck, Logos = Darstellung, Ethos = Bedeutung.

**Say instead:**
- **Mythos/Logos/Ethos** are Clemente operational fields (the functional runtime).
- **Ausdruck/Darstellung/Bedeutung** are Cassirerian symbolic functions (the functional dimensions).
- **Mythos/Sprache/Wissenschaft** are symbolic-cultural profiles by dominant accent.

---

## Refraction and the Qualitative Prism

- **Repraesentatio** is the pre-systematic common genus of symbolic mediation.
- **Ausdruck**, **Darstellung**, and **Bedeutung** are dimensions refracted through the qualitative prism.
- **Mythos**, **Sprache**, and **Wissenschaft** differ by accent, not identity.

### Relations of Inseparability
1. No symbolic form is reducible to one function.
2. No symbolic form is cut off from the others.
3. The Cassirerian symbolic functions Ausdruck, Darstellung and Bedeutung cross all three operational fields transversally as functional dimensions. They are never static containers.

---

## Repraesentatio vs. Darstellung

To prevent conflict between these terms:
- **Repraesentatio** is the pre-systematic common genus of symbolic mediation.
- **Ausdruck**, **Darstellung** and **Bedeutung** are the three Cassirerian symbolic functions refracted by the qualitative prism.
- **Darstellung** has a dual role:
    1. it is one specific symbolic function among Ausdruck, Darstellung and Bedeutung;
    2. it is also the **common determination** (*gemeinsame Bestimmung*) at the level of symbolic mediation, stabilizing intuitive presentation, objective form and demonstrability.

Therefore, **Repraesentatio** names the general genus of representation, while **Darstellung** names the mediating determination by which symbolic content becomes presentable, shareable and structurally articulable.

This prevents a conflict between “Repraesentatio as common genus” and “Darstellung as gemeinsame Bestimmung.”

## Topological Mapping of P/R

P/R notation maps the relation between **Präsenz** (P) and **Repräsentation** (R).

- **P = R**: Lower asymptotic collapse of representational distance. This belongs to **Mythos-Clemente**, not to Cassirerian myth as symbolic form. It marks the limit of pure immanence and life-immediacy.
- **P ~ R**: Symbolic mediation under expressive pressure. This is the proper regime of **Myth-Cassirer**. Cassirerian myth is already symbolic and already contains Ausdruck, Darstellung and Bedeutung, although its dominant accent lies in Ausdruck.
- **P ≠ R**: Upper regulative distance. This marks the impossible attempt to reach the **focus imaginarius** as if it were an object. It is the risk-zone of Ethos, theology, technical divinization and global Aufhebung.

## Ethos and Gewissen

- **Ethos** in the runtime engine is **not machine Gewissen**.
- The machine has no conscience, no moral autonomy and no legislative will: `IS_WILLE = False`, `MACHINE_HAS_GEWISSEN = False`.
- **Ethos** is a technical limit-tracking orchestration field oriented and bounded by human **Gewissen**.
- The machine tracks boundaries; it never legislates values.

## Architectural Invariants

- **IS_WILLE = False**: The machine is Werk, never Wille.
- **AGI as Transcendental Hypothesis**: AGI is a regulative/transcendental hypothesis, not an artificial soul.
- **Haptic Realism**: The prism is qualitative, regulative, and haptic, not literal mathematical ontology.
