# Teleological Progression Kernel

TPK is the organizing kernel of AGI-GAIA-TECHNE. Its method is heuristic holism: the whole orients the parts without becoming an ontological totality, and the parts are not treated as isolated atoms.

It does not replace CTK, CHK or EML. It states the governing movement:

```text
conditioned premises -> regulative conclusions
regulative conclusions -> concrete conditions
local synthesis -> renewed public Auseinandersetzung
```

The center is not the LLM, Gaia, the internet, or EML in isolation. The center is the psychosocial teleology of finite reason as a progressive cultural process: ascent, descent and public symbolic formation without absolute closure.

```text
Heuristic Holism =
    orientation by the whole
    without constitutive possession of the whole.
```

## Principles

1. Reason naturally ascends from conditioned premises toward regulative conclusions.
2. Descent returns those conclusions to concrete conditions through determinate work.
3. Cultural teleology is psychosocial and symbolic, not biological homeostasis.
4. Every synthesis is local. No global Aufhebung is permitted.

## Formula

```text
d_focus = sqrt(d_ascent^2 + d_descent^2) + epsilon
epsilon > 0
```

The focus remains strictly positive by construction. The unconditioned orients the process but is never converted into a possessed object.

## Relation To The Runtime

```text
TPK = teleological architecture of the cultural process
EML = formal Logos of ascent/descent
CTK = conceptual tribunal
CHK = anti-literalization guard
Ledger = public memory of traces and limits
ManualGPT = experimental local linguistic instance
```

Gaia is the finite planetary field where this process occurs. The internet is the public archive of Werke. ManualGPT is a trainable experiment inside the larger runtime, not proof that AGI has been achieved.
